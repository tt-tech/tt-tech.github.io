Join Our Team

We are looking for the best, therefore we are willing to offer you the best in terms of working conditions. We have experienced that a lot of highly qualified engineers are strongly motivated to succeed in their career, but the current labor market in Morocco does not match their expectations and wishes. We believe a radical reform of corporate mentality is necessary, and we are going to play a role in this. For us, our engineers are our most valuable assets.

We propose that you work within an outsourcing company where we work with European clients. In this way you get the chance to work with European partners on innovative products without the need to leave your own country.

In terms of working conditions, we promise you a fair salary that grows according to your experience and performances. We eagerly stimulate your professional development and support you once you wish to follow certain additional courses or attend a conference.

As we ask you to be flexible in your work, we offer you our flexibility in return. Besides the regular meetings, we give you the freedom to organize your working times yourself. We uphold a 40- hours workweek that is reduced during the fasting month of Ramadan. Once you have grown in our team, flexibility in working from home is an option.

We invite you to work at our office, to strengthen our team and share knowledge.

In our vision, a team is a fusion of efforts to believe in a mission and achieve its goals. We strive to form our organization as much as possible in a non-hierarchical way, as we believe in equality of our team members. It’s only together that we can succeed.

We endeavor to keep our organization open and transparent. In case of any problems we ask you to discuss them without any hesitation. Together we can find a solution for the problems you face. We also appreciate feedback from our engineers with regard to improvements that can be implemented. We openly discuss what goes good and what goes wrong, and together take responsibility. In this way we hope to create a working environment that is pleasant for everyone.
