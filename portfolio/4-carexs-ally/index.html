Tech Stack: Angular | React Native
Project: CareXS Ally

CareXS Ally replaces manual, error-prone processes in anticoagulation care and ensures a secure, digitized process . CareXS Ally supports the blood collection service with the fully digital processing of INR values ​​and notifications. And for venous collection with the digital processing of communications. Data is immediately visible to the dosing department. He can get started sooner, and the dosing advice is digitally available to the client more quickly. CareXS goes one step further by making the digital dosing calendar transparent for treating healthcare providers. That is very safe if you work with risk medication.
